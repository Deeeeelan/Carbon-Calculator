Carbon Emissions Calculator

About this project

Carbon emissions are the release of compounds containing carbon into the atmosphere.

Why are they harmful?

These compounds trap heat in the atmosphere, leading to global warming and climate change.
What is the purpose of the calculator?
This carbon emission calculator tells you how many carbon emissions you release using either a car, plane,, or train based on how many miles you traveled. It helps you become more aware of your impact on the environment, and changes you can make to decrease your carbon footprint.
